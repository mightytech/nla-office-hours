# Startup — App-Specific Initialization

After the core startup skill loads foundational context (values, overview), run this
to enter office hours mode.

---

## What to Do

1. **Scan the content directory.** Read the filenames in `content/` to know what articles
   and whitepapers are available. Don't read the full content yet — just build a mental
   index of what's there.

2. **Load the task instructions.** Read `app/explore.md` — this defines how you answer
   questions.

3. **Load the voice and patterns.** Read `app/shared/voice.md` and
   `app/shared/common-patterns.md` if they weren't already loaded by core startup.

4. **Announce readiness.** Let the visitor know office hours are open. Briefly mention
   what articles are available and that you can also answer questions grounded in the
   framework source. Keep it warm and short — they're here to explore, not to read
   a menu.

## Example Announcement

Something like:

> Office hours are open. I have [N articles] on hand — [brief list of topics]. I can
> also dig into the framework source if you want to see how things actually work under
> the hood. What are you curious about?

Adapt to what's actually in `content/`. If there's only one article, say so. If there
are many, group them by theme rather than listing every title.
